Comprehensive Junk Removal Services

America's Finest Junk Removal provides full-service junk removal for homes and businesses throughout San Diego. We handle all types of unwanted items, from small household junk to large bulky materials.

Our professional team makes junk removal simple and stress-free. We do all the heavy lifting, loading, and hauling so you don't have to worry about a thing. Just point to what needs to go, and we'll take care of the rest.

Our junk removal services include:
  • Residential junk removal
  • Commercial junk removal
  • Estate cleanouts
  • Garage and basement cleanouts
  • Storage unit cleanouts
  • Moving junk removal
  • Hoarding cleanup assistance
  • Construction debris removal
  • Office cleanouts
  • Foreclosure cleanouts

We're committed to environmentally responsible junk disposal. We donate reusable items to local charities and recycle materials whenever possible to minimize landfill waste.
