Construction Debris Removal Services

Remodeling your home or finishing a construction project? Let America's Finest Junk Removal handle the cleanup. We provide fast, efficient construction waste removal for homeowners and contractors throughout San Diego.

Our team will safely remove and dispose of all types of construction debris, leaving your property clean and ready for the next phase of your project.

Construction materials we remove:
  • Drywall, sheetrock, and plaster
  • Wood scraps, lumber, and trim
  • Concrete chunks (in manageable amounts)
  • Brick, block, and stone debris
  • Roofing materials and shingles
  • Metal studs, pipes, and ducting
  • Tile, ceramic, and porcelain scraps
  • Flooring materials (wood, laminate, vinyl)
  • Cabinets and vanities
  • Countertops (laminate, tile, stone)
  • Doors and door frames
  • Windows and window frames
  • Insulation materials
  • Sinks, toilets, and bathroom fixtures
  • Light fixtures and ceiling fans
  • Paint cans (empty and dried)
  • Construction packaging waste
  • Renovation debris and mixed materials
Common construction cleanup projects:
  • Kitchen and bathroom remodels
  • Home additions and renovations
  • Deck and patio demolitions
  • Basement and attic refinishing
  • Garage conversions
  • Fence removals
  • Interior wall removals
  • Flooring replacement projects
  • Post-construction cleanup
  • Commercial renovation waste

We sort and recycle construction materials whenever possible to minimize the environmental impact of your project.
