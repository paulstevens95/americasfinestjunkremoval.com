Refrigerator & Freezer Disposal Services

Replacing your refrigerator or freezer? America's Finest Junk Removal can safely remove and dispose of your old cooling appliances. Our team is experienced in handling these heavy items and will ensure they're disposed of responsibly.

Refrigerators and freezers contain hazardous materials and coolants that require special handling. We ensure these appliances are processed properly to prevent environmental damage.

We take all types of cooling appliances:
  • Full-size refrigerators
  • Compact/mini-refrigerators
  • Chest freezers
  • Upright freezers
  • Wine coolers and beverage fridges
  • Commercial refrigeration units
  • Deep freezers
  • Refrigerated display cases

Our team will remove refrigerators and freezers from any location in your home, including basements, garages, and tight spaces. You won't have to worry about the heavy lifting or navigating narrow doorways.
