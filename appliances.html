Appliance Disposal Services

Need to get rid of old appliances? Whether it's a refrigerator, washing machine, dryer, dishwasher, or stove, America's Finest Junk Removal can help you dispose of your unwanted appliances quickly and responsibly.

Our team handles all the heavy lifting, so you don't have to worry about moving large, bulky appliances. We'll safely remove your old appliances from any location in your home, whether it's upstairs, in the basement, or in your garage.

Appliances we take include:
  • Refrigerators and Freezers (all sizes)
  • Washing Machines and Dryers
  • Dishwashers and Garbage Disposals
  • Ovens, Stoves, and Ranges
  • Microwaves and Toaster Ovens
  • Air Conditioners (window and portable units)
  • Water Heaters (gas and electric)
  • Small Appliances (blenders, mixers, etc.)
  • Range Hoods and Exhaust Fans
  • Wine Refrigerators and Coolers
  • Ice Makers and Ice Machines
  • Furnaces and Heaters
  • Trash Compactors
  • Dehumidifiers and Humidifiers
  • Vacuum Cleaners
Common appliance disposal scenarios:
  • Home renovations and kitchen remodels
  • Upgrading to new, energy-efficient appliances
  • Moving to a new home
  • Estate cleanouts
  • Rental property cleanouts
  • Broken or non-functioning appliances
  • Business and office equipment removal

We're committed to environmentally responsible disposal. When possible, we donate working appliances to local charities or recycle them to minimize landfill waste.
