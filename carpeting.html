Carpet & Rug Removal Services

Replacing your carpets or getting rid of old area rugs? America's Finest Junk Removal makes carpet disposal simple and hassle-free. We'll remove and haul away all types of carpeting and rugs from your home or business.

Our team will handle all the heavy lifting, removing carpeting from any room in your home. We'll roll it up, carry it out, and load it into our truck - you won't have to lift a finger!

Types of carpeting and flooring we remove:
  • Wall-to-wall carpeting (all sizes and types)
  • Area rugs and oriental rugs
  • Carpet padding and underlayment
  • Carpet tiles and modular carpeting
  • Indoor/outdoor carpeting
  • Runners and stair carpets
  • Commercial carpeting
  • Vinyl flooring and linoleum
  • Laminate flooring materials
  • Damaged hardwood flooring
  • Rubber flooring and gym mats
  • Carpet remnants and scraps
Common carpeting removal situations:
  • Home renovations and remodeling projects
  • Floor replacements and upgrades
  • Water-damaged carpeting removal
  • Moving to a new home
  • Rental property cleanouts
  • Estate cleanouts
  • Office and commercial space renovations
  • Moldy or damaged carpeting disposal
  • Post-construction cleanup

We're committed to responsible disposal practices. When possible, we'll recycle your old carpeting to minimize environmental impact.
