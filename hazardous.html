Household Hazardous Waste

  • Paint Thinner - Up to 10 Gallons
  • Wood Stain - Up to 10 Galolons
  • Motor Oil - Up to 10 gallons (40 quarts)
  • E-Waste - Price per item. See chart below
  • Tires: $10/tire

A surcharge may be applied for any hazardous waste that is leaking or rusty

We are unable to take any medical waste. This includes, and is not limited to, discarded blood, sharps (needles, blades, etc.), used bandages/dressings, discarded gloves, or anything that has been in contact with blood or bodily fluids.
